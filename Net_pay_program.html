NET Pay Program

Calcuator for a person Net Pay and Gross Pay with the option to deduct additional amount from your check and displays it

  1. START
  2. Display "Please Input Your Gross Pay"
  3. DECLARE grossPay = "30000"
  4. DECLARE fedTax="0.15"
  5. DECLARE stateTax=".05"
  6. DECLARE ficaTax ".0762"
  7. PROCESS grossPay * fedTax
  8. Display "This is your NET Pay after the Federal deduction
  9. Output netPay
  10. PROCESS grossPay * stateTax
  11. Display "This is your NET Pay after the State deduction
  12. Output netPay
  13. PROCESS grossPay * ficaTax
  14. Display "This is your NET Pay after the FICA deduction"
  15. Output netPay
  16. END
